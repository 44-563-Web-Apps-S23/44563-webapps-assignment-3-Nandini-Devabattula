how to win friends and influence people

How to Win Friends and Influence People is a 1936 self-help book written by Dale Carnegie. This book basically tells you how to behave, react or handle the day to day life situations in a unique way which helps you to influence people or make them like you.The book contains 30 principles to be followed to make friendship instead enmity, to win deals instead of quarrel and misunderstanding.
